  • Next steps you can do at home

Services

Health Checkup

Understand your laptop’s current health — slow performance, heating, battery issues, and overall performance.

During the call, we'll guide you step-by-step to check temperatures, storage health, background activity, and performance issues. Based on the results, we'll explain what you can fix at home and what requires a technician referral.

Basic Fan Cleaning

Dust buildup blocking airflow? We guide you through cleaning your fans safely at home.

If your laptop is overheating or noisy due to clogged fans, we’ll walk you through a careful, safe cleaning procedure over a call — no expensive tools required. Ideal if you want to learn and do it yourself.

RAM Advice & Upgrade Help

Not sure if your laptop really needs more RAM? We help you decide.

If your laptop feels slow or struggles with multitasking, we’ll guide you to check how RAM is being used, explain what it means, and suggest whether an upgrade actually makes sense — so you don’t waste money.

Storage Advice & Upgrade Help

Running out of space or dealing with slow storage? We help you understand your options.

We guide you to check your current storage, explain the difference between HDDs and SSDs, and suggest whether a storage upgrade is worth it for your use — so you don’t spend money unnecessarily.

Others

Not sure which service fits your problem? You can still reach out.

If you have a laptop issue that doesn’t clearly fall into the categories above, feel free to contact us. We’ll listen to your problem, tell you honestly whether we can help, and guide you on what to do next. If it’s something that can be handled through advice, we’ll arrange a call.

Things About Us That You Should Know

✦ We help people who are confused about laptop problems and don’t know whom to ask.

✦ Our goal is to help you understand your laptop better — its health, performance, issues, and what to do next.

✦ You don’t need to pay first. Payment will be asked only after we confirm that we can help.
