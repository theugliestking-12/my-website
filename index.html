Dr. Laptop

Tired of confusing repairs and unclear advice? Get fast, friendly laptop help — diagnostics, cleaning, upgrades and quick troubleshooting for an affordable price. I’ll guide you through fixes and show how to keep your laptop running smoothly.

Services

Health Checkup

Understand your laptop’s current health — slowness, heating, battery issues, and overall performance.

During the call, We'll guide you step-by-step to check temperatures, storage health, background activity, and performance issues. Based on the results, we'll explain what you can fix at home, what settings to adjust, and what maintenance steps you should follow. If the problem requires tools or physical repair beyond what can be done online, we’ll clearly tell you and recommend visiting a technician.

Basic Fan Cleaning

Dust buildup blocking airflow? We guide you through cleaning your fans safely at home.

If your laptop is overheating or making noise due to clogged fans, we’ll walk you through the full cleaning process step-by-step over a call. You don’t need professional tools — we show you how to open the device carefully, remove dust, and improve airflow using simple and safe methods. Perfect if you want to clean it yourself and learn the correct way.

Upgrading RAM / Storage

Don’t waste money buying the wrong RAM or SSD — let us help you choose the right one.

Every laptop has specific requirements for upgrades, and choosing the wrong part can be a costly mistake. During the call, we’ll help you check exactly what your laptop supports and guide you toward the correct RAM or storage option to buy — simple, safe, and without any guesswork.

Advice on Buying a Laptop

Get personalized guidance on which laptop fits your needs, budget, and long-term use.

During the call, we help you understand exactly what kind of laptop you should buy based on your work, gaming, studies, or general use. We also explain what important things you must check before buying — so you don’t get confused by specs or marketing tricks. You’ll know what to look for, what to avoid, and which models make the most sense for you.
