Dr. Laptop

Tired of confusing repairs and unclear advice? Get fast, friendly laptop help — diagnostics, cleaning, upgrades and quick troubleshooting for an affordable price. I’ll guide you through fixes and show how to keep your laptop running smoothly.

Services

Health Checkup

Understand your laptop’s current health — slowness, heating, battery issues, and overall performance.

During the call, we'll guide you step-by-step to check temperatures, storage health, background activity, and performance issues. Based on the results, we'll explain what you can fix at home and what requires a technician referral.

Basic Fan Cleaning

Dust buildup blocking airflow? We guide you through cleaning your fans safely at home.

If your laptop is overheating or noisy due to clogged fans, we’ll walk you through a careful, safe cleaning procedure over a call — no expensive tools required. Ideal if you want to learn and do it yourself.

Upgrading RAM / Storage

Don’t waste money buying the wrong RAM or SSD — let us help you choose the right one.

We’ll help you check compatibility and guide you toward the correct upgrade options for your laptop so you don’t guess.

Advice on Buying a Laptop

Get personalized guidance on which laptop fits your needs, budget, and long-term use.

During the call we’ll help you match the right laptop to your use-case and show you what to check before buying so you don’t get fooled by specs-speak.
